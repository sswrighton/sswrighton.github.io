--- layout: post title: Silence in my office date: '2007-05-04T08:53:00.000-05:00' author: Stephen Wrighton tags: - Misc modified_time: '2007-05-04T08:59:12.103-05:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-947968907697915479.post-9092244671965551309 blogger_orig_url: https://programmer.wrighton.org/2007/05/silence-in-my-office.html --- I got a second LCD monitor for my office at work, so now I'm flying high with two of the things. They're quite nice and pretty and bright, and I'm enjoying them immensely. I also got a new PC. I had had the previous one for just shy of 3 years now. But what amazes me about this thing is that I can't hear it.

At all.

If I didn't know better I would swear this thing just doesn't have a fan in it or a heatsink, it's just that quiet. Of course that means I have to spend a decent amount of time reconfiguring the machine to work for me. I use a lot of custom settings in all the applications that I use on a consistent basis. But that's a fun all of its own.
